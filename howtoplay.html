How to Play

Controls

←→↑↓ Arrow keys to move
WASD Alternative movement
Left Click Shoot lasers
P Pause game

Objective

Survive as long as possible while destroying enemies and avoiding collisions. Collect power-ups to enhance your abilities!

Enemies

Fast Scouts

Fast moving green aircrafts. Worth 20 points.

Heavy Tanks

Slow moving yellow tanks. Worth 50 points.

Boss

Powerful red boss. Worth 100 points.

Energy Pulses

Fast moving energy balls. Worth 25 points.

Power-ups

Health

Restores one life

Shield

Temporary invincibility

Speed Boost

Faster movement
