About Galactic Pulse

The Story

In the distant future, cosmic energy pulses threaten the galaxy. As a brave pilot, you must navigate through these dangerous waves while defending against asteroids and energy formations that could destroy your ship.

Your mission: Survive as long as possible, collect power-ups to enhance your abilities, and set new records for future pilots to challenge.

Game Features

  • Fast-paced space combat
  • Multiple enemy types and patterns
  • Power-ups and upgrades
  • High score leaderboard
  • Customizable difficulty settings
  • Stunning neon space visuals
